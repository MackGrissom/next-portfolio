Dock Appeal is a Shopify-powered e-commerce store specializing in premium dock and marine accessories. The store features a custom theme designed to showcase products effectively and drive conversions.

## Project Highlights

- **Custom Shopify Theme** — Tailored design that reflects the brand's premium positioning
- **Optimized Product Pages** — High-converting layouts with detailed product imagery and descriptions
- **Streamlined Checkout** — Reduced friction in the purchase flow for higher conversion rates
- **Mobile Commerce** — Fully responsive design optimized for mobile shoppers

## Technical Implementation

Built on the Shopify platform with custom Liquid templates, the store balances visual appeal with e-commerce best practices. Product photography integration and strategic UX decisions create a seamless shopping experience.
