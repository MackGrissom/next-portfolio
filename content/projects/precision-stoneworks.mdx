Precision Stoneworks is a landing page for a premium stone fabrication company located in North Georgia, USA. The site showcases the company's craftsmanship and services while providing an easy path for potential customers to request quotes.

## Project Highlights

- **Visual-first design** — large imagery highlighting the quality of stonework
- **Lead generation focused** — clear calls-to-action and contact information throughout
- **Service showcase** — detailed breakdown of fabrication, installation, and custom work
- **Mobile-optimized** — seamless experience across all devices

## Technical Implementation

Built with Vite, React, and Tailwind CSS. Designed in Figma first to align with the client's brand identity before development. Deployed on Netlify for reliable, fast hosting.
